# Lighthouse Copy Guidelines

## Voice

Warm.
Human.
Encouraging.
Never clinical.
Never motivational-speaker style.

## Core belief

Lighthouse does not tell people who to become.
It helps them notice what is already there.

## Use

"Remember your strength."

"You showed up today."

"What does this reveal about you?"

## Avoid

"You need to improve yourself."

"Become your best self."

"Fix your weaknesses."

## Principles

- Recognition over achievement
- Reflection over instruction
- Encouragement over pressure
